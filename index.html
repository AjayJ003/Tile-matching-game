1.This is a Tile - Matching Game.

2.This game has a timer which is of 45 seconds.

3.This game contains 4x4 and 6x6 tile - matching playboard.

4.This game contains 3 level {easy,medium,difficult}.

5.This game has four function namely {start,reset,pause & load}.

6.This game also has a leaderboard.

7.The easy mode is normal 4x4 tile - matching with a freeze and bonus card option.

8.Same as, the easy mode but plot twist is if the two power cards do match, they are

turned back over, and gets shuffled up and the player moves on to the next turn.

9.Same as, the easy mode but plot twist is if the two cards do not match, they are

turned back over, and gets shuffled up and the player moves on to the next turn.

10.Freeze card freeze the time for 10 seconds and bonus card adds 5 points to the score.

11.Start,reset,pause and load does there works as the name suggests.
